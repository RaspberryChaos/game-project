How to Play

Welcome to Whale of Fortune!

It's like Wheel of Fortune, but underwater and with added sea creatures - what's not to love!

This is a game for 2 players. Click the 'Start' button to begin, then each player will be asked to enter their name and choose an avatar. Then it's time for the fun to begin!

The aim of the game is to solve the puzzle at the top of the screen. The category of the puzzle is shown just underneath.

Spin the wheel, guess a letter

When it is your turn you will spin the wheel then guess a letter to see if it appears in the puzzle. You can only guess consonants. The wheel will spin and stop at a random value and if you guess a letter correctly you will win that many points for each time that particular letter appears in the puzzle.

If you guess a letter correctly, you can spin the again and guess another letter until you have a wrong answer.If you guess a wrong letter, it will be the next players turn.

Solve the puzzle

When you know the answer, you can solve the puzzle by clicking the 'Solve Puzzle' button. If your answer is correct you will win the bonus shown to the bottom right of the puzzle. The bonus will decrease by €100 each time there is a wrong answer. You will then proceed to next next round. The player who won the last round is still the active player.

Player 1 goes first. Click the 'Spin the Wheel' button to begin.

Buy a Vowel

If you are stuck, you can buy a vowel. This will cost €500 from your round score. You can only do this when your round score is more than €500.

Lose a Turn

If the wheel lands on lose a turn, it is the next player's turn.You do not get to guess a letter or score any points.

Bankrupt

Oh no! If the wheel lands on bankrupt, you will lose all the money from your round score. The money in your total score is still safe however.

Winning

The winner is the player with the highest total score after 3 rounds.
